ABOUT

Use the CN Arcade app wherever you watch Cartoon Network shows to collect your favorite characters from Teen Titans GO!, The Amazing World of Gumball, Steven Universe, Ben 10, Craig of the Creek and more! Beast Boy, Gumball, Unikitty, Garnet, Raven, Four Arms and all the characters you love are waiting for you! Collect characters from every Cartoon Network episode you watch! New characters are added every month. Can you find them all? Smash pinatas, punch rainbows, kick soccer trolls, and play awesome games in the Cartoon Network Arcade. The better you play, the faster you’ll unlock exclusive figures. Game on! Become an arcade Legend! Earn high score ranks in every game to win Neons. Spend your Neons in the Shop to get the figures you want! There’s always something new to explore! Check back every week for new figures, events or activities.
