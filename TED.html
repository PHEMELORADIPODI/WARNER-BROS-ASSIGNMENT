TED LASSO

ABOUT

JASON SUDEIKIS returns as Ted Lasso, a small-time college football coach from Kansas hired to coach a professional soccer team in England… despite having absolutely no experience whatsoever coaching soccer. In season two, AFC Richmond is back on the pitch, albeit relegated to a lower division, with seven consecutive draws and no wins. And while assistant coaches Beard (series star BRENDAN HUNT) and Nate (series star NICK MOHAMMED) are itching not to hit the panic button, the team's seven-tie streak means something else to the eternally upbeat Ted Lasso – they're still undefeated!
